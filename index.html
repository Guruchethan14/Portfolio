Projects

Safe Ride: Next Generation Car Safety Solution

Safe Ride introduces a groundbreaking car safety system integrating Arduino technology. It features weather response with automatic wiper activation and fog light control for adverse conditions, object detection for collision prevention, alcohol detection to deter impaired driving, adaptive response to oncoming vehicle headlights, blind spot monitoring, and ultrasonic sensors for parking assistance. This project not only enhances vehicle safety but also demonstrates the potential of IoT in revolutionizing driving technology.
